Pizza

Description

Pizza is a universally adored Italian creation built on a thin, hand-stretched or pan-style dough base that bakes to a crisp, golden crust. It's typically layered with a tangy tomato sauce seasoned with garlic and oregano, then blanketed in creamy, melting mozzarella (or other regional cheeses). From classic Margherita with fresh basil to hearty meat-lover's or veggie-packed gourmet pies, the endless topping combinations let every slice cater to individual tastes. Served straight from the oven, pizza's warm, chewy bite and perfectly blistered edges make it a comforting, shareable meal enjoyed around the world.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (enough for a 12-14″ pie)
  • 2-3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza or tomato sauce
  • 8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano or Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • Your choice of toppings (e.g., pepperoni slices, sliced mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475 °F (245 °C), placing a pizza stone or inverted baking sheet on the middle rack if you have one.
  2. Stretch or roll the dough on a lightly floured surface into a 12-14″ circle (or rectangle), leaving a slightly thicker rim for the crust.
  3. Transfer the shaped dough to a piece of parchment paper or a well-floured pizza peel.
  4. Brush the outer rim with olive oil, then lightly season the entire surface with a pinch of salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  5. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, stopping about 1/2″ from the edge.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella over the sauce, then dust with oregano or Italian seasoning.
  7. Arrange your desired toppings over the cheese in a single layer.
  8. Slide the pizza (on the parchment or peel) onto the preheated stone or baking sheet.
  9. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  10. Remove, let rest for 2-3 minutes, then garnish with fresh basil leaves, slice, and serve hot.
